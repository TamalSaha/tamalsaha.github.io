System Design and Analysis

The goal of this project was to study and analyze the accounting system of a real world organization and find its dataflow and shortcomings. We designed and implemented an efficient system that overcomes the shortcomings and fulfill the requirements of its users and owners. The organization, we will analyze for this project in this course is one of the largest media consulting group in Bangladesh, ASIATIC MEDIA GROUP.
